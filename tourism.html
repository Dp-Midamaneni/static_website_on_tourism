Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is considered to be the greatest architectural achievement in the whole range of Indo-Islamic architecture. Its recognised architectonic beauty has a rhythmic combination of solids and voids, concave and convex, and light shadow. Not a piece of architecture, as other buildings are, but the proud passions of an emperor’s love wrought in living stones.
